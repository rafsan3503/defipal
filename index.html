We Offer Best Debit Cards

How To Get a physical Card

1. Login or register on our platform (https://dashboard.defipal.org/);

2. Click "My cards" on the Left Side;

3. Click "Apply for physical card" on the upper right side;

4. Complete the requestal with your personal information and wait for 24-48 hours to get your KYC approved;

5. Buy your DefiPal card once you are approved;

6. Your physical card will be shipped directly to your home, it takes days or weeks based on your location.
